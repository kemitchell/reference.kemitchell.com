## Chinese Cleavers

- Slicers (caidao)
  - thinnest
- Choppers
  - medium thickness
  - most common
- Cleavers (gudao)
  - thickest

- common
  - 18 cm to 28 cm
  - carbon steel
  - ground bevel
  - full, think tang bent over back of handle

- leads
  - Three Rams brand
